- **Website:** veritasbuildersgroup.com

### 2. Ask Every Past Client for a Google Review

Reviews are the second-highest local SEO signal. A contractor with 20+ Google reviews dominates the local 3-pack against competitors with 0–5.

**Get your review link:** In GBP → Home → "Get more reviews" → Share review form. Shorten it with bit.ly and save it in your phone.

**Text script (send the day of completion or next morning):**
> "Hi [Name], it was great working on your [kitchen/deck/etc.]. Really glad you're happy with how it turned out. If you have 2 minutes, an honest Google review would mean a lot — it helps other homeowners find us. Here's the link: [short link]"

**If no response in 3 days, one follow-up:**
> "Hey, just checking in — hope you're loving the new [kitchen/etc.]. No pressure, but if you get a chance: [link]"

**Target:** 10 reviews in the first 90 days. At 10+ reviews the listing gains significant ranking authority. At 25+, competitors can't displace you.

Do not offer incentives for reviews (violates Google's TOS). Ask for an "honest review," not a positive one.

### 6. Jefferson County Chamber of Commerce

**Cost:** ~$100–200/year
**ROI:** Highest referral value of any paid action in a small rural county.

Membership gives:
- Business directory listing (citation)
- Networking events with local decision-makers
- "New member" spotlight in newsletter/social
- Relationships with other local businesses who need construction work

Show up to the first few events in person. This is how most revenue flows in the first two years for a local contractor.

---

### 7. Build a Referral Network

Word-of-mouth and referrals will outperform digital for the first 2 years. The highest-value relationships to cultivate:

**Real estate agents (top priority)**
Every home sale generates renovation, repair, and "make it showable" work. Contact the North Florida Association of Realtors (NFAR) — their events are more efficient than cold outreach. Offer to be the agent's reliable contractor they can call.

**Insurance adjusters and public adjusters**
Jefferson County is in a hurricane-prone region. Storm and water damage work is steady and pays well. Reach out to local State Farm, Allstate, and independent agencies to get on their preferred contractor lists.

**Property managers**
Jefferson County has rentals and vacation homes that need ongoing maintenance contractors. Search for local property management companies and introduce yourself.

**Home inspectors**
Inspectors see deficiencies in every inspection and refer contractors constantly. Find Jefferson County home inspectors on the FL DBPR lookup (HI license type).

## Optional Paid — Only After GBP Has 10+ Reviews

### 8. Google Local Services Ads

**What:** Pay-per-lead ads appearing above all organic results with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. You pay only when a prospect calls or messages.

**Cost in this market:** ~$15–75 per lead (rural FL trends lower). At $150–300/month expect 5–15 leads.

**Verdict:** Best paid option for a solo contractor. Do not start until GBP is verified and has 10+ reviews — reviews directly affect LSA ranking.

**Setup note:** Requires a one-time background check (~$25–35) and license verification. CBC1266699 speeds this up.

### 9. Skip Angi / HomeAdvisor Paid Leads

Leads are sold simultaneously to 3–5 competing contractors. Close rate is 10–20%, putting customer acquisition cost at $333+ before time spent chasing. A solo operator can't win the speed-to-contact competition against larger operations with dedicated sales staff.

Use the free profiles for citation value only.

## V2 Website Additions

These require development time but are high SEO value:

### 10. Service-Area Location Pages

One page each for:
- Contractor Tallahassee FL
- Contractor Madison FL
- Contractor Perry FL
- Contractor Quincy FL
- Contractor Crawfordville FL

Each page: H1 = "[Service] in [City], FL", 300–400 words mentioning the city, services list, link to contact. These rank for "[city] + contractor" searches and are the fastest path to organic Tallahassee traffic.

### 11. Project Gallery / Before & After Page

A `/projects` page with photos from completed jobs. Every photo should have an alt text and caption mentioning the location (e.g. "Kitchen remodel in Monticello, FL"). This is the highest-converting content for contractors.

### 12. Wire the Contact Form

The current form shows a success message but sends nothing. **Formspree** is the easiest option for a Vercel static site:
1. Sign up at formspree.io (free tier handles 50 submissions/month)
2. Update the form's `action` attribute with the Formspree endpoint
3. Submissions go directly to `admin@veritasbuildersgroup.com`
